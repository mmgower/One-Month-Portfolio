Megan M. Gower

Writer, Poet, Teacher

Megan is a poet and writer based out of Greensboro, North Carolina. After recieving her BA in English at Appalachian State University, she attended the Columbia Publishing Course and worked in publishing as a marketing assistant and remote editor before deciding to return to NC for her MFA in poetry at UNCG. She has experience teaching college writing and literature courses, and has helped students actualize their writing projects as a writing consultant. Her poetry has been featured in The Greensboro Review, The Applachian Review, Pembroke Magazine, and The Main Street Rag.
